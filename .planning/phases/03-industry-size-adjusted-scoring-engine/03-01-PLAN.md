<objective>
Implement ENG-02 (industry weight adjustment) and ENG-03 (company-size
normalization) exactly per the design spec's "Scoring engine" section: a
static per-industry multiplier table applied to the base category weights
then renormalized to 100, and Technology/Automation coverage ratios scaled
by an employee-count-band baseline instead of the full absolute checklist
length.

Purpose: This is the core of Phase 3 — it is what makes two submissions
with identical raw ratings produce different, context-appropriate final
scores for a manufacturer vs. a SaaS company, and stops small companies
from being judged against enterprise-scale absolute counts. Both are pure,
well-defined business logic with clear input/output contracts, hence TDD.
Output: `lib/scoring/industryWeights.ts` (new), and `calculateScore.ts` /
`categoryFormulas.ts` extended in place — all proven by passing automated
tests before Plan 03-03 wires the results page's user-visible confirmation.
</objective>
