My recommendation for KODRYX

Don't build this first as a PowerPoint. Build it as a digital assessment platform.

Phase 1: A web application with 120–150 structured questions, automatic scoring, and AI-generated recommendations.

Phase 2: An internal consultant dashboard where your team reviews, refines, and approves the AI-generated insights before they are shared with the client.

Phase 3: One-click generation of a professionally branded PowerPoint and PDF report, complete with maturity score, SWOT analysis, opportunity matrix, ROI estimates, department-wise findings, and a customized implementation roadmap.

This approach turns the assessment into a scalable product rather than a one-off consulting exercise, allowing every KODRYX consultant to deliver a consistent, high-quality executive assessment.
